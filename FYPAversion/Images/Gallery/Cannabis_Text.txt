

Also known as
Marijuana, Pot, Grass, Joints, Ganja, Hashish, Weed


Description

Cannabis comes from the hemp plant. It can come in various forms such as "loose" cannabis leaves, compressed blocks, corn cob-shaped, oil, etc.


Dangerous Effects

-Inability to concentrate
-Slow reaction
-Distorted thinking and perception
-Poor balance and co-ordination
-Extreme anxiety, depression, confusion and paranoia
-Decline in motivation and drive in long-term users
-Moodiness, irritability, anxiety and tension


Withdrawal Symptoms

Moodiness, irritability, anxiety and tension
